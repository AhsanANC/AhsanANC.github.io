Frequently asked questions

Answer common questions here.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Orders can usually be modified or canceled within a short window after purchase. If you need to make a change, please contact our support team as soon as possible with your order number and details. We will do our best to accommodate your request, but once an order has been processed or shipped, changes may not be possible.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you’ll receive a tracking number via email to monitor your shipment.

What is your return policy?

We offer hassle-free returns within 30 days. Items must be in their original condition and packaging.

How long does shipping usually take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5–7 business days. Expedited options may be available at checkout.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! We offer digital gift cards that can be purchased directly from our store. You can choose the amount, and the gift card will be sent via email to the recipient instantly. Gift cards are a convenient way to give someone the flexibility to pick the items they love, and they never expire within the valid period.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping fees, delivery times, and customs charges may vary depending on your location. International shipments may take longer to arrive than domestic orders, so please allow extra time for processing and delivery. You can track your order once it has been shipped using the tracking number provided in your confirmation email.
